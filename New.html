Point to remember
  • Please note thaat all cheques which are apporved from the DDO Cheacker/Officer/govt from 01/03/19 shall get paid through E-kuber which is a core banking Solution of RBI.
  • There is no need to present the cheques at all the bank for these cheques which got apoorved after 01/01/2019.
  • Please give the coorect account details as it is when the Acount was opened.
  • Make sure there is no "Your self" or "Self" in the account name while issuing cheques.Such cheques get auto rejected by the E-kuber.
  • Please the exact length of the Bank Account Number and No special chracter are to be entered ,which leads to auto-rejection.
  • Finally,in Multiple party Cheques Please do not enter the same party details in the same chequeno which will be considered as a duplicacy Transaction and gets auto rejected.
  • This is just one-time procedure to get your party details corrected and once when corrected the same details can be re-used for smooth transcations.
  • PD-to-PD cheques shall be adjusted in tresury itself in the regular procedure.
